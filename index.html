MacArthur Park in Lawton, Oklahoma

MacArthur Park is a 400 home subdivision within Lawton, Oklahoma.
It is adjacent to MacArthur Middle School and MacArthur High School, and situation near multiple elementary schools.
The community has its own pool, playground, open fields, and tennis court.

Mission Statement

The MacArthur Park Association provides maintenace, preservation, and architectural control of the residence lots and common area within MacArthur Park and to promote the health, safety, and welfare of the residents whith MacArthur Park and any additions thereto as may hereafter be brought within the justrusditiciton of this association.
